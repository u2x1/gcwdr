## How Captions Display

The caption text appears in **two places**:

1. **Tooltip** - Hover your mouse over any image to see the caption as a browser tooltip
2. **Below Image** - The caption also displays directly under the image in styled text

## Caption Styling

Captions are displayed with:

- **Smaller font** (90% of normal text size)
- **Italic style** for visual distinction
- **Gray color** (#666) for subtle appearance
- **Centered alignment** matching the image
- **Responsive** - adjusts on mobile devices
